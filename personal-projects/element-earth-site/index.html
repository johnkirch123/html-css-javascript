Element Earth is fully committed to the wholesale and retail distribution of quality and unique climbing holds and volumes. We want to connect climbers with the world’s best climbing lines; classics and first ascents that gyms can now offer to patrons in their own backyard. Brought to you by the world’s greatest climbing athletes, now anybody can Climb Around The World & Climb With the Pros.

We offer holds that are cost effective, quality, and are perfect replicas of actual outdoor wall features. At Element Earth our goal is to keep gym climbing exciting, and get climbers interested in new lines around the world. Through industry leading proprietary 3-D replicating methodology, we hope to bring the “outside, indoors.”

- Nicholas Diederich
