John Kirch

What can I say, I am super passionate about web design and development. I got my start in programming when a need arose to manage our inventory at a pharmaceutical company that I worked at. I thus embarked on a journey to learn how to design, build, and deploy this program using java and I was hooked! I often spend as much available time as possible to learn about the industry and new technologies as well as spending a lot of time developing projects for both friends and myself.


Currently I am getting my skills to a point that I will completely comfortable with all aspects of full stack development and have decided to focus mostly on the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, and Nodejs). I am currently solidifying my skills on the front end before moving to the back end, although I have had experience with API's, SQL, AWS, and a few other back end technologies.
